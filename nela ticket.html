Nela Ticket

Nela Ticket is a 2018 Telugu action-drama where Ravi Teja, a carefree man, takes on a corrupt politician, Aditya Bhupathi, seeking justice for the oppressed. The film mixes action, comedy, and emotion, emphasizing good triumphing over evil.
